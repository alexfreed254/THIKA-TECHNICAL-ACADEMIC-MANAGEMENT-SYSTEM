Select class, unit and lesson below then click Start. Fingerprint sensors in the room go live — trainees scan their fingers and you see instant updates on screen.

How it works
1. Choose your class, unit and lesson details below.
2. Click Start Biometric Session — this opens the live attendance screen.
3. Trainees place their fingers on the sensor in the room — each scan appears on your screen in real-time with a green tick.
4. Override any scan manually if needed, then click Save Attendance — records go into the same attendance database as manual entries.
